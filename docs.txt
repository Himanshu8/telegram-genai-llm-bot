Company Policy:
Employees are allowed to work from home up to two days per week with manager approval. All employees must maintain productivity and attend scheduled meetings.

Office Hours:
The standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Employees are expected to be available during these hours unless otherwise approved.

Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to 20 paid leave days per year. Leave requests must be submitted at least 3 days in advance through the HR system.

IT Support:
For technical issues, employees can contact IT support via email at support@company.com or call the helpdesk. Support is available during working hours.

Password Reset:
To reset your password, go to the account settings page and click on “Reset Password.” Follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

Security Policy:
Employees must not share their passwords with anyone. All systems must be logged out when not in use. Unauthorized access is strictly prohibited.

Remote Work Guidelines:
While working remotely, employees must ensure a stable internet connection and maintain communication with their team via official channels.

Meeting Policy:
All employees must attend mandatory meetings. If unable to attend, prior notice must be given to the team lead.

Expense Reimbursement:
Employees can claim reimbursements for approved expenses by submitting receipts through the finance portal.

Code of Conduct:
Employees must maintain professional behavior at all times. Harassment or misconduct will lead to disciplinary action.

Recipe Guide:
To cook pasta, boil water, add salt, and cook pasta for 8–10 minutes. Drain and mix with sauce before serving.

Customer Support:
Customer queries should be addressed within 24 hours. Escalate unresolved issues to senior support staff.

Training Policy:
Employees are encouraged to complete at least one training program per quarter to improve their skills.

Data Privacy:
All employee and customer data must be handled securely and in compliance with company policies.
